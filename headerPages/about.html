MrHarryW – Developer, Innovator, and Digital Creator


Introduction

Technology is a constantly evolving field, and I have always been fascinated by how software can bring people together, automate tasks, and create meaningful experiences. My journey as a developer began with a simple curiosity about how things work, leading me to build my first Python-based Snake game in 2022. Since then, I have continued developing my skills, working on a diverse range of projects—including custom Discord bots, web applications, and developer tools—all aimed at improving efficiency, fostering collaboration, and enhancing digital experiences. As a self-driven developer, I specialize in Python, HTML, and JavaScript, leveraging these languages to create tools that benefit online communities. Over time, I have not only built projects for my personal growth but have also contributed to open-source development, ensuring that others can learn from and improve upon my work.



Discord Bot Development & Automation

  • Watch;Dog - The steward of "; semi-colon", my developer-focused Discord community. Watch;Dog is more than just a simple bot; it is an essential pillar of moderation, order, and information sharing. It ensures that the community runs smoothly, preventing spam, enforcing rules, and even providing automated updates. What makes Watch;Dog stand out is that it is open-source, allowing developers within the community to analyze, modify, and contribute to its ongoing development. The source code is publicly available on my website for those interested in learning from or enhancing its functionality.
  • Ticket Time - A multilingual Discord ticketing system, enabling efficient customer support and issue resolution within Discord communities. Unlike traditional ticketing bots, Ticket Time supports multiple languages—currently English, Spanish, French, Arabic, and German, with planned expansions into Russian, Mandarin, and more. This bot has been incredibly useful for server administrators who manage large communities.

These bots have significantly improved automation within Discord communities, reducing the workload for moderators and enhancing user experiences.



Building a Community for Developers – "; semi-colon"

One of my proudest achievements is the creation of "; semi-colon", a rapidly growing Discord community for developers. The goal of this server is to bring together individuals from diverse technical backgrounds—whether they specialize in coding, 3D modeling, game development, cybersecurity, AI, or other digital fields.

"; semi-colon" is designed to be a collaborative hub where developers can:

  • Share projects and get feedback from peers.
  • Find resources to enhance their coding skills.
  • Discuss technical challenges and problem-solve together.
  • Collaborate on open-source initiatives.

To ensure that "; semi-colon" remains organized, secure, and productive, I have developed several custom bots—most notably Watch;Dog, which plays a crucial role in moderating discussions, enforcing rules, and managing automated announcements. This bot is open-source, allowing other developers to explore its code and contribute improvements.


As "; semi-colon" continues to grow, I am committed to expanding its reach, introducing new learning resources, and building more advanced automation tools to support the community.

Future Goals & Next Steps

Looking ahead, I am excited to continue pushing the boundaries of development. My future projects will focus on:

  • Expanding "; semi-colon" into a leading hub for developers, designers, and digital creators.

From my early Python projects to building a developer community, my journey has been one of constant learning, problem-solving, and innovation.
