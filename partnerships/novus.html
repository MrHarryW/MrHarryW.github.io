Novus: The Rise of an Empire


In Novus, you begin with nothing—just a rundown room, a handful of supplies, and a relentless ambition to rise above the filth of the streets. But in this city, success isn’t given; it’s taken. From the moment you step into the underground world, every decision matters. Do you take the safe route, making slow and steady gains, or do you risk it all for a shot at fortune? One wrong move, and everything you’ve built could come crashing down.



From the Bottom to the Top


Starting from the slums of Novus, your empire begins humbly. Your first operation is small—a single hideout where you cook product in secrecy. The money comes in fast, but so do the dangers. The streets are unforgiving, and the competition is brutal. Other dealers want you gone, the law is always lurking, and the people closest to you might sell you out if the price is right. The road to dominance is paved with blood, money, and betrayal.



Expanding Your Reach


As your wealth and influence grow, so do your opportunities. Upgrade from makeshift labs in abandoned buildings to full-scale manufacturing plants hidden behind legitimate businesses. Smuggle goods across the city through underground networks, bribe officials to keep the cops at bay, and recruit trusted enforcers to protect your empire from rivals. With every expansion, your risk increases—larger operations attract more attention, and the authorities will stop at nothing to bring you down.



Rivals, Betrayals, and Wars


You’re not the only one with ambitions in Novus. Rival factions control different parts of the city, each with their own turf, suppliers, and enforcers. Step into their territory, and you’ll either need to negotiate, buy them out, or start a bloody war. But beware—alliances are fragile, and betrayal is just another business strategy. Even your closest allies might turn on you if they see a better deal elsewhere. Stay one step ahead, or find yourself at the mercy of those who once called you ‘friend.’



Managing the Business


Running an empire isn’t just about making money—it’s about keeping it safe. You’ll need to launder your earnings through legitimate fronts, maintain a steady supply chain, and ensure that law enforcement stays off your back. Balance the risks of cutting corners for fast profits against investing in high-quality production to dominate the market. Hire dealers to push your product, but keep a close eye on them—some might get greedy, others might get caught, and a few might be working with the cops. Snitches must be dealt with swiftly, or they could bring everything down.



Law Enforcement and Corruption


The police are always watching, and the higher you climb, the harder they’ll push to take you down. Small-time dealers get overlooked, but major operations attract task forces, undercover agents, and federal investigations. Bribing the right officials can buy you time, but it won’t last forever. The law adapts—new technology, surveillance, and informants will challenge your every move. If they get too close, you’ll need to find a way to eliminate the threat, whether it’s through intimidation, blackmail, or something more permanent.



The Open World of Novus


Novus is a living, breathing city where every corner tells a different story. From the slums and industrial zones to the high-rise districts and secluded mansions, every area presents new opportunities and threats. Control smuggling routes through back alleys, hidden tunnels, and corrupted shipping docks. Expand into high-end clientele, dealing to politicians, executives, and celebrities, but be careful—high-profile deals mean high-profile risks.



A Dynamic World of Consequences


Every choice you make shapes the world around you. Increase your presence too quickly, and the law will respond with force. Mistreat your workers, and they might turn on you. Cut deals with one faction, and another will seek revenge. The underground economy is ruthless, and only the smartest, most ruthless, and most adaptable players will survive. Will you rule the city as an unstoppable crime lord, or will you make one fatal mistake that brings your empire crashing down?



The Ultimate Crime Empire Experience


  • Start from nothing: Build your operation from a single hideout to a sprawling criminal enterprise.
  • Expand your empire: Upgrade from small-scale production to full-scale factories and distribution networks.
  • Manage risk vs. reward: Cut corners for quick profits or invest in high-quality production to dominate the market.
  • Deal with rivals: Negotiate, buy out, or eliminate those who stand in your way.
  • Handle law enforcement: Bribe officials, eliminate snitches, and evade ever-evolving police strategies.
  • Control the streets: Take over smuggling routes, black markets, and the entire underground economy.
  • Experience a living world: A massive open-world city filled with opportunities and dangers.

One wrong move, one bad deal, one betrayal—that’s all it takes to lose everything. But for those willing to take the risk, the rewards are limitless.


Will you rise to power and rule Novus, or will you be just another name lost to the streets?
