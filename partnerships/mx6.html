Partnership with MX6 Studio


We are excited to announce our collaboration with MX6 Studio, a new 3D and 2D art publisher for game developers and content creators. Their passionate team of students specializes in creating models and UI for various projects.



About MX6 Studio


MX6 Studio is dedicated to supporting game developers and content creators by providing high-quality 3D models and user interfaces. Their commitment to excellence and innovation positions them as a valuable resource in the industry.
