Bringing THE Obby Back: Restarting Development on My Obby After Two Years

Two years ago, I started working on a Roblox obby called Sunshine Obby (just changed to THE Obby), a fun and challenging obstacle course designed to test players' skills. Life got busy, and I stepped away from development, but now, I'm excited to announce that I'm restarting work on the game!

Why Now?

Over the past two years, I’ve gained more experience in game development and digital marketing, and I feel ready to apply these skills to THE Obby. With Roblox continuously evolving, there are so many new features and possibilities to explore that can make the game even better.

My Plans for THE Obby

  • Revamping the Levels – I'll be tweaking existing levels to improve gameplay and ensure a smooth difficulty curve.
  • Adding New Stages – More challenges, unique mechanics, and surprises to keep players engaged.
  • Improved UI & Visuals – A fresh new look with polished UI elements and enhanced graphics.
  • Quality of Life Updates – Bug fixes, performance improvements, and better checkpoint mechanics.
  • Community Engagement – I plan to take feedback from players and implement ideas that make the game more enjoyable for everyone.

New Gamepasses & Features!

I'm also excited to introduce brand-new gamepasses that will enhance the gameplay experience for players who want some extra fun. These include:

  • Flying Carpet – Soar through the obby with a flying carpet and enjoy a bird’s-eye view of the course.
  • Tommy Gun – Wield a Tommy gun and add an action-packed element to your obby experience.
  • Music Player – Customize your in-game experience by playing your favorite tunes while you navigate the obstacles.
  • Speed Coil – Move at lightning speed with a speed coil, allowing you to complete stages faster than ever!

These gamepasses will give players unique ways to interact with the game while still keeping the challenge alive for those who want the traditional experience.

Stay Tuned!

This is just the beginning of the next chapter for THE Obby. I’ll be sharing development updates along the way, so keep an eye out for new features, sneak peeks, and release announcements.

If you haven’t played THE Obby yet, check it out here: THE Obby. Your feedback and support mean a lot, and I can’t wait to bring this project back to life!
