What is Ticket Time?

Ticket Time is a Discord bot that helps developers and server administrators efficiently manage support tickets. With the ability to open, close, and track tickets in multiple languages, Ticket Time ensures a seamless user experience on your server.

Whether you use it for technical support, general inquiries, or specific concerns, Ticket Time makes communication easier and ensures no request is overlooked. The bot provides an easy-to-use interface and high customization to meet the needs of any server.

Why German for Ticket Time?

Our community is growing constantly, and it was only a matter of time before we wanted to offer Ticket Time in German. Many Discord servers, especially in German-speaking regions, needed a solution that better reflected their needs and requirements. With the introduction of the German language, we hope to support even more people in managing their servers and improving support for their members.
